welcome to tech for V I

  1. 1. After joining the group, it is mandatory to provide your introduction within 24 hours, including your full name, country, city, and the device you use.
  2. 2. Before asking any question, you must mention your name, device name, and Android version; and if your question is about a computer, you must mention the manufacturer and the Windows version, and explain how to use the screen reader.
  3. 3. Keep your voice message clear, lower the volume of your screen reader, address the issue point-to-point, and limit your conversation strictly to the question at hand, avoiding any off-topic chit-chat.
  4. 4. If you don't receive a response to your question or app request within 24 hours, you may repeat your message after that time. However, if you repeat it within 24 hours, you will receive a warning, and if you still continue to do so, you will be removed from the group.
  5. 5. Always treat others with respect, avoid unnecessary arguments, and when replying to a user, make sure to select their message first so they know it is a reply. Always speak with patience and refrain from using any offensive language.
  6. 6. Sharing any kind of articles, application links, or social media links is strictly prohibited in this group, unless a member has specifically requested a link for an application or tutorial. If you share links without a request, you will be removed from the group immediately.
  7. 7. Direct messaging any group member without their prior permission is strictly prohibited. If you wish to contact another member, you must ask for their consent within the group first. If you make personal contact without permission and the other member complains to the admin, you will be removed from the group immediately without warning.
  8. 8. Once you receive a reply, be sure to react to that person's message with a thumbs up or red heart, and there is no need to send separate welcome or thanks messages.

Please note that before you click the 'Join group' button, you must read all these rules. Also, after you join, the admin might ask you about any of these rules to ensure you have read them.
